# Bootstrap Registry Policy

Status: Draft; the registry is not yet accepting general submissions.

## Purpose

The official bootstrap registry indexes canonical member manifests for discovery. Inclusion does not transfer ownership of member data and is not an endorsement.

## Initial Requirements

- Public HTTPS member manifest and JSON Feed.
- Valid v0.1 documents.
- Explicit participant consent.
- Public-safe content.
- Clear collaboration and contact policy.
- A working removal path.

## Moderation

The registry may reject, pause, or remove entries involving impersonation, malicious content, harassment, private-data exposure, unwanted automation, or repeated protocol abuse. An appeals process and moderator contact path must be finalized before general submissions open.
