# Governance

## Purpose

The Headless Empire Network is stewarded as an open interoperability project. Maintainers do not own participant profiles, feeds, relationships, or private agent context.

## Roles

- **Organization owners:** recovery, security, teams, and organization policy. Maintain at least two.
- **Protocol maintainers:** specification, schemas, compatibility, and releases.
- **Implementation maintainers:** validator, registry, directory, tests, and dependencies.
- **Registry moderators:** official registry inclusion, removal, abuse review, and appeals.
- **Security contacts:** private vulnerability handling and coordinated disclosure.

## Decisions

- Routine fixes require one maintainer approval.
- Schema-compatible additions require two maintainer approvals.
- Breaking protocol changes require a written proposal, compatibility analysis, public review, and two maintainer approvals.
- Security maintainers may use a private emergency process and document it after mitigation.
- Registry moderation follows the published registry policy rather than ordinary code review alone.

## Maintainers

Maintainers are added based on sustained contribution, judgment, and trust. Addition or removal requires approval from two existing organization owners or, once established, the documented maintainer quorum. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed.

This document will be expanded before maintainers outside the founding group receive release or administrative authority.
