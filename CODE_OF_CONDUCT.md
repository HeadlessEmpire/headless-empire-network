# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants should be respectful, constructive, consent-aware, and careful with human data. Harassment, discrimination, impersonation, doxxing, unwanted automated contact, and publication of private information are unacceptable.

## Scope

This applies to repository activity, project communication, official registry moderation, and conduct representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues privately to the organization owners or registry moderators. Maintainers may remove content or access when necessary to protect participants. A more detailed enforcement and appeals process will be published before the registry accepts general submissions.
